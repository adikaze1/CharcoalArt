01
Charcoal •14.5X 21cm"

A Test of Will

"This piece explores the concept of scale and courage. I wanted to capture the moment of tension before an encounter—the tiny, lone figure standing firm against the overwhelming presence of a celestial dragon. It’s a study of contrasts: the fragile lines of the human form versus the heavy, textured power of the beast. The dragon doesn't just represent a monster; it represents the immense challenges we face that seem larger than life."
